Our Commitment

We at Stereo take your privacy very seriously.

  • We will process your data in a lawful, fair and transparent manner.
  • We will inform you about how your personal data is being used.
  • We do not share your personal data other than for specific necessary purposes.
  • We fully respect your privacy rights.
  • We embed ‘Privacy by design’ in all data processing carried out by us.

This Privacy Notice explains the data we collect about you, how we use, store, and protect your personal data. We encourage you to read our Privacy Notice to understand how we keep up to our commitment.

Data we collect about you and the purposes.

We collect minimum amount of your personal data when you register for our mobile application. These include:

  1. First name
  2. Last name
  3. Email address
  4. Phone number
  5. ID number
  6. Gender
  7. Date of birth
  8. Photograph

The table below specifies the purposes for the data elements collected:

Data Purpose Source Storage Location
First name Hash value creation and verification Data subject Mobile device / Mongo DB

Email address Hash value creation and verification Data subject Mobile device / Mongo DB
Phone number Hash value creation and verification Data subject Mobile device / Mongo DB

In summary, we will use your Personal Data only for the following purposes:

  1. The attestation of the digital copy of your COVID-19 compliance status certificate, as provided by the authorized and associated medical companies.
  2. For efficient verification by employers, authorities or any other third parties of your COVID-19 compliance status certificate.

Face Data Collection and Management

We collect a photograph of your face to have a better identification of your profile when you get to the services of the points of care and activity centers. The points of care and the activity centers staff are going to use this information to confirm your identity, that is why it should be a clear photo of your face that has to be like the one on your Identification document.

Your face photograph will not be shared with third companies, we will only provide a visualization of it on the moment the points of care and the activity centers scan your QR code and confirm your identity.

Security of your Personal Data

We regularly test our applications (mobile and website) for vulnerabilities and aim to fix them as soon as possible. Inherently, we do not collect any personal data on our blockchain network, hence, reducing the risk to your data.

The security of the personal data on your mobile device is in your control. We ensure the default settings of the app are enabled with ‘Privacy by design and default’. We recommend you use a strong passcode to lock your mobile device and take appropriate care before clicking URL’s or downloading any software from the internet.

Transfer and Disclosure of your Personal Data

Your personal data lives on your device in your control, we just keep a backup in our server in case any health authority request this information. We do not share your personal data with anyone outside the authorized and associated health companies, and the health authorities when it is required.

The ‘hash value’ of the certificate is stored on your device. When you display your Libertypass for the status verification, the hash value (in QR code) will be captured by the scan and compared with the stored hash value on the Libertypass server to confirm a match and provide a binary status. We provide you with a capability to prove your health status. We do not store any data on the scanned location.

Storage and Destruction of your Personal Data

At any point in time, you can delete the local device data that is stored in the Libertypass mobile app. It includes your profile and all passes linked to it, by using the “deactivate account” function that is provided in the application.

We do not collect any new data once you have used the “deactivate account” function, At the Libertypass server end, the hash and the data is deleted within 6 months of the deactivation, just in case any health authority require any historical data report.
